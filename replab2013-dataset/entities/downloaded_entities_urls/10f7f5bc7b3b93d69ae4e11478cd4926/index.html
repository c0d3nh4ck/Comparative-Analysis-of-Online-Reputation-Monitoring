HSBC Water Programme

HSBC is investing USD100 million to help transform the lives of more than one million people through providing access to safe water and spearheading water protection projects.

The five-year programme, launched by HSBC Group Chairman Douglas Flint, involves working in a groundbreaking partnership with WWF, WaterAid and Earthwatch.
